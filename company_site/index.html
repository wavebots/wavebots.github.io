Our story

Welcome to Funny and Company. Our founder, Funny, had a vision in 1818 where every person on earth was smiling at the same time. He knew he could not make it happen. So he settled on making anybody who comes to his circus keep their smiles on, long after they left the show.
In keeping with Funny's vision, several generations of the troupe members were able to keep this tradition, keeping the smile on your face for the rest of the week.
